Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and deliver personalized content and ads.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics: To track visitor behavior (such as pages viewed, session duration, and location) and improve our website.
  • Google AdSense: To display personalized ads based on your interests and location.
  • Geolocation: To provide location-specific content and services (only with your explicit consent).

3. Types of Cookies Used

Service Cookie Name Purpose Duration
Google Analytics _ga, _gid Visitor analytics 2 years
Google AdSense _gcl_au, IDE Ad personalization 3 months
Geolocation geo_data Location-based content Session

4. Your Consent & Controls

We require your consent for non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Adjust your preferences via our cookie consent banner.
  • Block cookies through your browser settings.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Manage AdSense personalization through Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Sharing

Data from these cookies may be shared with:

  • Google (for analytics and advertising)
  • Geolocation providers (for location services)

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via our website.
